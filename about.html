About Camping Products Sales

Your trusted outdoor adventure partner since 1995

Our Story

Founded in 1995 by outdoor enthusiasts Mark and Sarah Thompson, Camping Products Sales began as a small family business with a simple mission: to provide high-quality camping gear that enhances outdoor adventures.

What started as a single retail location in Colorado has grown into a trusted name in outdoor equipment, serving thousands of adventurers across the country. Our commitment to quality, customer service, and the outdoor community remains unchanged.

Today, we're transitioning into the digital age while maintaining the personal touch and expertise that our customers have come to expect. Every product we sell is tested by our team of outdoor professionals.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To inspire and equip outdoor enthusiasts with premium gear that enhances their adventures while fostering a deeper connection with nature.

Our Journey

Nearly three decades of outdoor excellence

1995
The Beginning

Mark and Sarah Thompson open their first camping store in Boulder, Colorado, with a focus on high-quality gear and expert advice.

2001
Expansion

Opened second location in Denver and began offering guided camping workshops and outdoor education programs.

2008
Recognition

2015
Innovation

2024
Digital Future

Launching our comprehensive online platform while maintaining our commitment to personal service and expert advice.
